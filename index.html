About

I am a sound engineer and composer who enjoys photography and web development. I enjoy creating music and making games. I am currently focusing on developing my skills in these areas through continued education. With the concert / entertainment industy on a temporary hiatus, I believe now is the time to finally learn to type without looking.. and enter the world of web development. I have built this site from the ground up and am more amazed by the world of coding with each new trick that I learn. Seems like we can learn anything from the comfort of home with an internet connection and a little bit of ambition, and that is awesome!

Sound Engineer

I have over 15 years experience producing, writing, recording, editing, mixing and mastering in studio as well as mixing live bands at concerts and festivals with crowds between 1 – 15000 people.

  • As a child in Vancouver, I experimented with my parents VCRs, 8mm video camera and record player, making rudimentary films with overdubbed sound.
  • As a teenager I began to play guitar, before making it my goal to collect as many different instruments as possible.
  • I have been working with DAW’s and computer audio since my first 386 DOS computer.
  • In 2003, during my time studying music and sound at The Arts Institute of Vancouver I began working with large PA systems at concerts and events.
  • After completing my studies I continued to work as a freelance engineer while learning about cinematic music production and building a library of music.
  • I am very happy and grateful to have had the chance to work with every one of the amazing artists and engineers over these past years!

Music Producer
